About Me

I am a Master’s student in Data Science at Stevens Institute of Technology, driven by a passion for transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. My academic journey began with a Bachelor’s in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, where I developed a solid foundation in statistical modeling, machine learning, and big data analytics.

At Stevens, I actively engage in practical, results-driven projects, including developing a hybrid job recommendation system integrating content-based and collaborative filtering, fine-tuning a LLaMA-3 model for structured SVG prompt generation, and implementing dimensionality reduction techniques for credit risk assessment. These experiences demonstrate my ability to combine advanced machine learning techniques with real-world datasets to deliver impactful, data-driven solutions.

My professional experience spans internships in machine learning and data analytics, where I designed predictive models, optimized classification algorithms, and developed interactive dashboards that improved business decision-making. With proficiency in Python, R, SQL, Tableau, and cloud platforms like AWS, I specialize in building scalable AI solutions that deliver measurable impact.

I aspire to leverage my technical expertise and research acumen to contribute to cutting-edge AI applications in industry and academia, while continuing to explore innovative ways to bridge the gap between data and decision-making.
